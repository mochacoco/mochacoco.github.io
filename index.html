About

I received my M.S. (2020) and B.S. (2018, cum laude) from Seoul National University. I completed my Ph.D. in the Soft Robotics and Bionics Laboratory (SRBL), advised by Prof. Yong‑Lae Park. My research focuses on probabilistic modeling, Bayesian filtering, and learning‑based control for soft robots and visuo‑tactile systems. I care about explainability and stability in ML‑for‑robotics.

Keywords: soft robotics, state estimation, system identification, MRAC, Gaussian processes, real‑time vision.

Research Areas

Probabilistic State Estimation

Bayesian filtering and uncertainty‑aware sensing for compliant robots and visuo‑tactile systems.

Learning‑based Modeling & Control

Data‑efficient identification, stability‑aware policies, and MRAC for soft actuators and continuum robots.

Real‑time Vision for Robotics

Stereo depth, homography‑based 2.5D warping, and fast perception for surgical and lab robots.
